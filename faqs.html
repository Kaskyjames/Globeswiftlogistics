Frequently Asked Questions

Shipping & Tracking

How do I track my package?

Visit our Track page and enter your tracking number or reference code to see real-time updates.

What locations do you serve?

We ship domestically within the USA and internationally to over 150 countries worldwide.

How fast can you deliver?

Local deliveries can be same-day. International shipping depends on destination and customs clearance.

Do you provide shipment insurance?

Yes, we offer optional insurance coverage for added protection against loss or damage.

What if my shipment is delayed?

Delays may occur due to customs or weather. You can contact our support team for help with any issues.

Booking & Payments

Can I book a delivery online?

Yes! Use our Booking page to schedule a pickup or shipment.

How is shipping cost calculated?

Pricing is based on package weight, dimensions, destination, and delivery speed.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers.

Can I modify or cancel a booking?

You can request changes by contacting us as soon as possible before dispatch.

Do you offer volume discounts?

Yes, we provide special rates for frequent shippers and bulk orders.

Customs & Documentation

What documents are required for international shipping?

Typically, a commercial invoice, packing list, and any required permits or certificates. We will guide you through this.

Can you help with customs clearance?

Absolutely, our team can assist with customs declarations and paperwork to ensure smooth clearance.

Are there restricted or prohibited items?

Yes, certain goods like hazardous materials or perishable items may be restricted. Please review our shipping guidelines or contact us.

Do I need to pay import duties?

Import duties and taxes may apply depending on destination country regulations.

Support & Policies

How can I contact customer support?

You can reach us via email, phone, or our Contact page for any inquiries.

What happens if my shipment is damaged?

Please report damage within 24 hours of delivery. If insured, you can file a claim for compensation.

Do you offer packaging services?

Yes, we provide professional packaging to protect your shipment during transit.

Is my data safe when booking online?

We use secure encryption to protect your information. Your privacy is important to us.

How can I set up a business account?

Contact us to learn about our corporate solutions and set up an account tailored to your shipping needs.
