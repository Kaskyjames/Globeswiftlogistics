Frequently Asked Questions

Shipping

How do I book a shipment?

You can book directly via our Booking page or by contacting our support team. We’ll guide you through the process.

What items are prohibited from shipping?

We do not ship hazardous materials, live animals, perishable foods, or items restricted by law. Check our full prohibited list before shipping.

Do you provide international shipping?

Yes, we ship to over 200+ countries across all continents using air, sea, and express courier services.

Can I schedule a pickup?

Absolutely. You can choose a pickup date and location when booking your shipment.

How long does shipping take?

Shipping duration depends on the destination and service type — local delivery may be same-day, while international shipping can take 5–10 business days.

Tracking

How can I track my shipment?

Use the Track page on our website. Enter the required information to access your shipment status.

Can I receive delivery notifications?

Yes. You can opt-in for real-time updates via email or SMS when submitting your tracking request.

My tracking number isn’t working. What should I do?

Ensure the number is entered correctly. If the issue persists, please contact our support team with your reference number.

How often is tracking updated?

Tracking is updated in real time as your shipment progresses through our logistics network.

Can I track multiple shipments at once?

Yes. You can enter multiple tracking numbers separated by commas on our tracking page.

Payments & Pricing

How is the shipping cost calculated?

Costs are based on shipment weight, dimensions, packaging type, and delivery destination. Custom quotes are available.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit cards, debit card, bank transfers, payment link, cryptocurrency, and select local payment options depending on your region.

Can I request a custom quote?

Yes. For bulk or special shipments, please reach out to us with shipment details and we’ll provide a tailored quote.

Are there any hidden fees?

No. All charges are transparently displayed before you confirm your booking. Optional services like insurance may add to the total.

Do you offer discounts for frequent shippers?

Yes. We provide loyalty rewards and business account benefits for regular shippers and corporate clients.

Support & Documentation

Do I need to upload shipping documents?

Yes. Required documents may include invoices, identification, and permits depending on the type of shipment.

Can I speak to a live support agent?

Yes. Our customer service is available 24/7 through phone, email, and live chat.

What happens if my shipment is delayed?

We’ll notify you immediately and work with carriers to resolve delays as quickly as possible.

Do you offer insurance for shipments?

Yes, insurance is optional and can be added during the booking process to cover against loss or damage.

How do I cancel or change my booking?

You can cancel or modify your booking from your account dashboard or by contacting us directly within the allowed window.
