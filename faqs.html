Frequently Asked Questions

Shipping

How do I book a shipment?

You can book directly on our Booking page or by contacting customer support.

What items are prohibited?

We cannot ship hazardous materials, live animals, or restricted goods. See our policy page.

Tracking

How can I track my shipment?

Use your tracking number on our Track page.

Can I get notifications?

Yes, opt-in for email/SMS updates when booking.

Payments

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Visa, MasterCard, bank transfer, and PayPal.

How do I get an invoice?

Invoices are emailed after booking and also available in your account dashboard.
