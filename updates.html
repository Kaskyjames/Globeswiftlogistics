How Shipment Updates Work

We make it simple to track, manage, and confirm your shipments without an account.

1. Smart Tracking Page

Enter your Tracking Number and Email or Phone to access live shipment details anytime. You can also download important documents like your Waybill or Invoice after verification.

2. Automated Notifications

Stay informed every step of the way. You'll receive instant Email and SMS alerts whenever your shipment status changes (e.g., Picked Up, In Transit, Delivered).

3. Order Confirmation Pages

After booking, you'll see a clear confirmation page summarizing your shipment. A copy of this confirmation is also sent directly to your email for your records.
